Plans for future releases
Here we list a few things that we plan to include in the future. We are very interested in your suggestions of useful improvements or additions to the code.

  • Use long long int and gmp for long integer arithmetics also in 'zsolve'.
  • sparse implementation of 'groebner' and 'markov'
  • binomial ideals (generated by a*x^b-x^c, no cancellation allowed)
  • computation of Hilbert bases from cone generators
  • weighted poincare series
  • computations on monomial ideals such as primary decomposition
