'markov' extracts a minimal generating set from a graded Gröbner basis.
Extract Markov basis from Gröbner basis in fileName.gro.

If "fileName.gro" does not exist, the project-and-lift algorithm is used to compute a generating set of the lattice ideal and to extract a Markov basis of it.
